Website Summary

What content will the site contain?

The website will contain a reference guide for video game collectors, including high-quality images of video game consoles, troubleshooting guides for common console issues, and pricing guides for games and hardware. It will also feature searchable data, side-by-side console comparisons, and historical pricing trends.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (i.e., images & text)?

The website will source images from official console manufacturers, historical archives, and user-submitted photos where permitted. Text content will be written based on extensive research from trusted collector resources, auction sites, and firsthand knowledge from experienced gamers.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

The website will provide valuable information to collectors, gamers, and enthusiasts who want to track prices, troubleshoot issues, and learn about rare or valuable gaming hardware. It will serve as a go-to reference for those looking to maintain, restore, or expand their collections.

Why should a site be built for this?

There are many forums and community-driven sites about gaming collections, but few dedicated reference sites that provide structured, easy-to-navigate information without requiring user interaction. This site will fill that gap by offering an organized and reliable source of data.

What would you like users to do at your site?

The website will allow users to browse console images, troubleshoot their hardware issues, compare console specifications, and check price trends. Users will be able to quickly find the information they need without digging through community posts or outdated forums.

What is the goal of the site?

The website aims to be a comprehensive reference for video game collectors, helping them make informed decisions about their collections, maintain their hardware, and track market trends. It will serve as a valuable tool for both new and experienced collectors.
